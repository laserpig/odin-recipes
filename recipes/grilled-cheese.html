Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Grilled cheese sandwiches may seem childish to some, but there's no reason sophisticated adults can't enjoy a tastefully done grilled cheese!

Our recipes covers a simple but hearty and satisfying grilled cheese we know you will love. Serve with hot tomato soup for a slam dunk!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread of your choice
  • Butter
  • Sliced cheese of your choice (cheddar works best)
  • Thin sliced tomato
  • Cooked rotisserie chicken
  • Onion
  • Fresh arugula

Steps

  1. Chop onion and sautee in a pan until wilted and translucent
  2. Melt butter in a frying pan over mediu heat
  3. Place two slices of bread in the pan and drop a slice or two of cheese on each
  4. Layer chunks of the cooked chicken, onion, and tomato slices
  5. Finally add arugula on top and cover the pan for one or two minutes
  6. Carefully place the empty slice of bread on top of the other one
  7. Enjoy
